You just arrived in the region of Kanto, this region is inhabited with small monsters that live in the bush... The Pokemons !!!
Incredible isn't it ?
Unfortunatelly, there are a bunch of people who wish to possess the strength of Pokemons, they are doing a lot of strange experiences on it... The Team Rocket !!!
After a wild party at the beach club of Vermilion City, you cross the path of James, one of the most famous Team Rocket's soldiers !
It's now time to fight, grab your pokemon and defeat the Ennemy !

Pokemon have different types, and each type has weaknesses and strength, here are the folowwing:

  • ROCK, is a threat for WATER but is threatened by PLANT
  • PLANT, is a threat for ROCK but is threatened by FIRE
  • FIRE, is a threat for PLANT but is threatened by WATER
  • WATER, is a threat for FIRE but is threatened by ROCK

You've just entered the game, but without any pokemons you can't start it, so here are the rules to help you with the game control process method:

  1. Pick your cards from the card deck on the right, it will show your cards and your ennemie ones, you will be able to pick five cards, not more, not less.
  2. Start the game with the "Start Game" button just below the card deck
  3. The game has started, each pokemon has a different speed, the fastest one starts
  4. Choose your attack with the keyboard arrows as mentionned below
  5. Fight and defeat James to spread peace in the Pokemon World
  6. GOOD LUCK !
